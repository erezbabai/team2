Shiatsu therapy

Shiatsu therapy is designed to open the blockages in the meridians, direct the energy and affect the muscular, nervous and internal systems of the body and thus bring about a balance of body and mind and improve the general feeling. it can lead to a process of self-healing in which the body releases chemicals with anti-inflammatory that facilitate movement and evoke a feeling of relaxation


Chinese acupuncture

Chinese acupuncture - is one of the cornerstones of ancient Chinese medicine. The treatment is performed by inserting delicate disposable needles on top of energy points in the body.


Reflexology

Reflexology is a method of diagnosis and treatment in alternative medicine. This method is based on a pseudo-scientific assumption that points and areas in the foot represent a "map" of all organs and systems of the body. Through observation and touch of the foot, defects in various body systems can be diagnosed and treated.
